# 💡 Business Insights & Recommendations

Analysis period: FY 2024 | Data: 45,312 transactions

---

## Key Findings

### 1. 📈 Electronics is the Growth Engine
- Revenue grew **+34% YoY** — highest of all categories
- Smartphones and audio accessories drove Q4 spike
- Q4 alone = 42% of annual Electronics revenue
- **Action:** Increase inventory & marketing budget 30% for Oct–Dec window

### 2. 🗺️ North vs South Region Gap
- North achieved **118% of annual target** (only region to exceed)
- South achieved only **71%** — biggest underperformer
- Root cause: 3 active reps in South vs 9 in North
- **Action:** Hire 4 reps in Karnataka & Tamil Nadu — projected +₹18L annual uplift

### 3. 👑 Platinum Customers Drive Disproportionate Revenue
- Top 5% customers = **38% of total revenue**
- Avg Platinum LTV = ₹2.4 lakh
- Retention rate = 82% (industry benchmark: 85%)
- **Action:** Dedicated account manager + early product access program → target 95% retention = +₹3.2M annually

### 4. 📅 December Festive Spike
- December = ₹3.2M (13% of annual revenue)
- 2.8× order spike during Dec 20–27 window
- Inventory stockouts in Electronics were recorded on Dec 22–23
- **Action:** Pre-stock Electronics by Nov 30, activate surge logistics capacity from Dec 15

### 5. 🔄 Churn Improved but 2,841 Customers Are At-Risk
- Churn: 8.5% → **6.4%** (attributed to Q2 loyalty program)
- 2,841 customers have not ordered in 180+ days
- **Action:** Win-back campaign with 15% discount → projected 18–22% reactivation = ₹42L revenue

### 6. ⚡ Operational Efficiency Gains
- Order processing: 48 hours → **22 minutes** post-automation
- Cancellations reduced by **31%**
- **Action:** Replicate warehouse automation playbook to South region DC
